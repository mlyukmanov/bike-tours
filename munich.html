Voice & Help

Get a natural-sounding voice (one time, free)

iOS has human-like voices that aren't installed by default:

  1. Open Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content → Voices → English
  2. Tap a voice marked Premium or Enhanced (e.g. Kate, Serena, Martha) — far more natural than the default.
  3. Download it, come back here, open this menu, and pick it above.

No sound?

  • Flip the ring/silent switch off and raise the volume.
  • Tap Play once directly (iOS needs a tap to allow audio).
